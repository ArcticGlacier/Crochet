WANT TO LEARN CROCHET?

Crochet is a craft that allows you to create textiles through the use of a single hook and some yarn. Although often confused for knitting, crochet has unique stitches that produce lively motifs and designs. Some advantages of crochet are that it is more beginner friendly than knitting, as it is harder to unravel projects accidentally. Along with this, stitches do not need to be passed back and forth between needles like they do in knitting. If this sounds unfamiliar or confusing, don't worry!
